# AdSense Checklist

## Before Applying
- Custom domain connected.
- HTTPS enabled.
- Home page complete.
- Tools page complete.
- Blog page complete.
- About page complete.
- Contact page complete.
- Privacy Policy page complete.
- Terms page complete.
- At least 5 useful tools.
- At least 10-20 useful articles or detailed tool pages.
- No broken links.
- Mobile responsive.
- Fast loading.
- No copied content.
- No prohibited content.
- Google Search Console connected.
- Sitemap submitted.
- robots.txt available.
- Website has clear navigation.
- Website has enough original value without ads.

## Do Not Do
- Do not click your own ads.
- Do not ask friends to click ads.
- Do not tell users to support you by clicking ads.
- Do not create fake traffic.
- Do not use misleading ad labels.
- Do not place ads too close to buttons.
- Do not make ads look like navigation.

## Recommended Ad Slots After Approval
- Below intro of long article.
- Middle of long article.
- End of article.
- Sidebar on desktop only.
- Between content sections, not inside tool controls.

## Bad Ad Slots
- Next to copy buttons.
- Inside input forms.
- Above a button where users may accidentally click.
- Popups that block the tool.
- Too many ads on short pages.

## AdSense Implementation Rule
In code, keep ads behind a flag:

`NEXT_PUBLIC_ADSENSE_ENABLED=false`

Only set it to true after approval.
